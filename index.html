Privacy Policy

Version 1.0.3 | Last Updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection

Labour Connect collects your name, professional skill category, and location (District/Ward) to help connect you with potential work opportunities. We use Firebase for secure authentication and data storage.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your profile information is shared within the app to facilitate job connections. We do not sell or share your personal data with third-party advertisers or external organizations.

3. Data Security

We take industry-standard measures to protect your information, especially your payment history and profile records stored via Google Firebase.

4. Account Deletion

Users have full control over their data. You can request permanent account and data deletion by clicking the button below.
